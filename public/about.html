About Connect More

Connect More was created by Matthew Ess, Jay Hankins, and Maxwell Molnar for CS 252: Systems Programming, Spring 2016 at Purdue University.

It is open source software based in part on C4 by Kenrick.

It is built in HTML5 and JavaScript, runs on Node and Express with MongoDB.
